Pitch

Face the judgment of winter’s deadly toll as you lead the first Viking clans to settle Iceland in this non-violent, rogue-like survival base builder.

Factsheet

  • Release in 2023
  • Platforms: Win, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS, Xbox, PlayStation, Switch
  • Developed & published by indie studio Sonderland

Description

The thrill of a dungeon crawler with the vibe of a city builder, Landnama is a non-violent, rogue-like survival base builder. You lead the first Viking clans to settle Iceland as time ticks down towards Winter's Toll.

A NON-VIOLENT VIKING GAME
There’s no combat or raiding, these are farmers, explorers, and builders, but you’ll need every bit of your focus to survive.

SERENE BUT DEMANDING
A taxing race against time with an ever present ticking clock pushing you always closer to winter in a beautiful, atmospheric world.

SINGLE RESOURCE, MANY PATHS
Only one resource exists in the game, Heart, that can be gained and spent in many ways, whether you want to focus on exploring the land as a hunting clan or by building your settlement into a thriving production hub.
