Landnáma Privacy Policy

We at Sonderland take privacy very seriously – but to make the best possible games we need to collect some data to improve our products and to operate our games. This privacy statement discloses which what information we collect and how we use it. By playing Landnáma, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this statement.

What data do we collect for which purpose?

  • Leaderboards: nickname, score, gameplay data
  • Balancing: gameplay data

We group data by a randomly generated user ID and do not collect IP addresses or other personally-identifiable information. Although we do use some third party services which may:

  • Google Play Services (Android Version)
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore (All versions)

About Links to Other Sites

This site contains links third party websites. We do not control these sites collect information. If you have questions about the data collection procedures of linked sites, please contact those sites directly.

Children’s Privacy

Landnáma does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

This policy is effective as of 2023-03-22

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about how we handle your privacy, do not hesitate to contact us at hello@sonderland.games.
